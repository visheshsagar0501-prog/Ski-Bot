Know your limits
When to see a specialist
Ski Bot is a screening aid, not a diagnosis. Reach out to a professional if you notice any of the following.

🩹 See a dermatologist if:

  • A mole is rapidly changing shape or color
  • A rash persists despite gentle care
  • You notice signs of infection or spreading
  • You have severe or worsening pain

👁 See an ophthalmologist if:

  • Sudden vision changes or blurriness
  • Eye pain, redness, or discharge
  • Halos around lights or eye pressure
  • Any trauma or injury to the eye

Ski Bot is an AI-assisted screening tool for educational purposes and does not replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a licensed dermatologist or ophthalmologist for any health concern.
